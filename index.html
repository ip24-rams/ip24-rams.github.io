Why I Love Concerts

Since around 2021, I have developed a love for concerts and live music. Being able to witness an artist perform their art live with such passion and love for what they do feels contagious as an audience member and stimulates my own personal happiness. Nothing compares to the feeling of being in a crowd full of people who all love the same music. Concerts make me feel connected, and I love collecting these moments that I can fondly reminisce about.
